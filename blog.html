How Avanti Frozen Foods Went from 63% to 74% On-Time Delivery

01
The Challenge

One planner. Three plants. Hundreds of interdependencies.

Avanti Frozen Foods operates at scale — 3 plants, 9 production lines, over 100 SKUs, multiple vendors, and approximately 2,500 tonnes of processing per month. The operation demanded constant coordination across species, packaging formats, changeover sequences, and vendor delivery windows.

And it was all being held together by one planner and a spreadsheet.

🏭

3 plants, 9 lines — each with unique machine constraints, line speeds, and SKU compatibility requirements.

📦

100+ SKUs across multiple species and packaging formats, each with different changeover requirements and vendor lead times.

⚠️

1 in 4 orders at risk of missing their delivery window. Capacity underutilised, materials misaligned, no visibility into the real bottleneck.

The planner isn't late because they're slow. They're late because the spreadsheet gives them no way to be on time.

— PatternLab implementation assessment, Avanti Frozen Foods
02
The Complexity

It's not a people problem. It's a planning problem.

When an operation is this interconnected, the relationships between decisions matter as much as the decisions themselves. Three questions that no spreadsheet can answer in real time:

?

Which plant can run which SKU — given line compatibility, material availability, and current capacity load?

?

Which line clears which changeover — and in what sequence to minimise downtime across the week?

?

Which vendor sends which material — and does the delivery window align with the scheduled run?

Answering each question manually takes time. Answering all three simultaneously, across three plants, is structurally impossible without a connected system.

03
The Resolution

Factory Brain: a connected scheduler built for complex operations.

PatternLab replaced Avanti's fragmented planning process with a single connected system — every constraint encoded, every decision traceable.

Live Capacity Planning

Real capacity visibility per plant, per line, per week — pulled directly from the scheduling engine. Overloads flagged before commit.

Automated Feasibility Checks

Every incoming order validated against live machine capacity and raw material availability in seconds. No surprises at execution.

Changeover-Aware Scheduling

An interactive Gantt that respects constraints — changeover times, line compatibility, and batch sequencing — accessible to the whole team.

Full Traceability

Every schedule version, plan change, and strategy choice logged automatically. Full visibility into what was planned, when, and by whom.

04
What Was Installed

One system replaced every spreadsheet, manual check, and undocumented rule.

The implementation covered the full planning workflow — from order intake to schedule release — across all three Avanti plants. No workarounds. No parallel processes.

Factory Brain gave the planning team live capacity visibility per plant, per line, per week pulled directly from the scheduling engine. Overloads were flagged before commit, not after the week fell apart. A structured planning cycle that didn't depend on one person's memory replaced the informal, tribal knowledge that had previously held the operation together.

05
Adaptation

Four pillars. No manual paperwork or spreadsheets.

Pillar 01

Smart Scheduling

Machines aligned to actual production hours. Idle equipment flagged automatically. Schedules built on real constraints, not assumptions.

Pillar 02

Full Digitalization

Every plant-floor planning process moved from manual oversight to continuous real-time visibility — across all three sites simultaneously.

Pillar 03

Unified Tracking

Every machine and material availability under one live view — what's running, what's available, what's at risk. No more parallel spreadsheets.

Pillar 04

Supply Chain Traceability

End-to-end visibility from raw material intake to final pack. Every batch tracked, every handoff logged, 100% digital and auditable.

Transformation Results

Nothing changed on the floor. Everything changed in the plan.

No guesswork. Only data and results.
